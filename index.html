Laid Off Calgary has evolved!

We now offer both in-person meetups (in small, facilitated peer support groups) and a confidential, closed online group on Facebook, for job seekers and people struggling with the downturn. If you are a job seeker or have been laid off from your job in Calgary, we offer support and help for the following:

  • Managing the stress and fear of your laid off experience
  • Understanding the psychological aspects of job loss and what you can do about it
  • Combatting the isolation and loss of self that can accompany unemployment
  • Strategies for job search and how to effectively manage your time during transition, and so much more...

Both the in-person and online Laid Off Calgary groups provide additional knowledge sharing, peer support and resources.

  • Many participants of Laid Off Calgary have since found work. Attending a group meeting doesn't guarantee a job but you will gain something different every time that can help you during your transition, cope better, pick up specific tips and resources, form new connections with other people and not feel so alone!

  • The group meetings are facilitated by trained volunteers. We employ different facilitation styles and coaching techniques to help you access your own inner wisdom and also provide a forum for listening to each other. Many members have new friends from the group and some have been able to help find others work.

  • We typically have a maximum of 12 attendees for each in person meeting, and there is always a mix of familiar faces and new. The smaller group format makes for more comfortable conversations. Our 90 minute meetings go very fast! Our meetings are very interactive and powerful learning opportunities. We also need to know the number of registrants so we we don't go over the room capacity so we do ask that people register their interest to attend following the links in eventbrite.com.
